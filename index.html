Shuoguang Yang

I'm an Assistant Professor in Department of Industrial Engineering and Decision Analytics, HKUST. I received my PhD in Operations Research from Columbia University (2020). During my time at Columbia, I was extremely fortunate to be supervised by Professor Van-Anh Truong and Professor Daniel Bienstock. Before going to Columbia, I got my bachelor's degree from National University of Singapore in Applied Mathematics (2014). My research focuses on revenue management, optimization and online learning.
