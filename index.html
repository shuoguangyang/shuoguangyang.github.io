Shuoguang Yang

I'm an Assistant Professor in Department of Industrial Engineering and Decisio Analytics, HKUST. I received my PhD in Operations Research from Columbia University. During my time at Columbia, I was extremely fortunate to be supervised by Professor Van-Anh Truong and Professor Daniel Bienstock. Before going to C, I got my bachelor's degree from National University of Singapore. I work on different problems in revenue management, optimization and online learning.
